COUNTDOWN TO LA FIESTA!

DOWNTOWN ELGIN, IL
SEPTEMBER 13, 2025
FREE EVENT!

La Fiesta de Elgin is back for its third year! Join us for a vibrant celebration of Mexican Independence Day on Saturday, September 13, 2025, in the heart of Downtown Elgin.

Get ready for a day full of music, food, culture, and community pride! The festivities kick off Saturday morning with a lively morning parade. The excitement continues at Festival Park with an evening celebration you will not want to miss.

PLAN YOUR DAY

La Fiesta de Elgin Parade | Saturday, September 13 | 9:00am

üìçDowntown Elgin

La Fiesta de Elgin Parade car staging begins at 7 a.m. at 901 Center St (old Sherman lot). Cars will step off at 9:00am at the intersection of Slade and Douglas Ave and ends at Douglas Ave and Highland Ave, near City Hall. Seating is available along Douglas Avenue... Vehicles or floats only! No walkers. For more information please reach out to the Friends of Masons.

Parking: All parking in Downtown Elgin is free, however, with street closures taking place for the parade, we advise that you arrive early. Besides street parking, Downtown Elgin offers plenty of free parking in lots and decks here.

El Mercado | Saturday, September 13 | 10:00am - 6:00pm

üìç Riverside Drive

We are turning up the energy with a special La Fiesta de Elgin Mercado. Stroll through a vibrant market featuring Latino-owned businesses, live music, a car show and family-friendly activities for all ages. From delicious eats to one-of-kind retail finds, there is something for everyone.

Want to be part of the fun as a food or retail vendor? Head over to our Vendor Page to apply!

A full list of entertainment and participating vendors will be shared soon!

La Fiesta de Elgin Celebration | Saturday, September 13 | 5:00pm- 10:00pm

üìçFestival Park | 132 S. Grove Elgin, IL 60120

Get ready for a vibrant night of culture, music art, and fun as Mexico comes to Festival Park! A stunning, large-scale Angel of Independence will welcome guests at the center sidewalk, setting the stage for an unforgettable evening. Additional vibrant art installations will make their return celebrating Mexican heritage. There will be plenty of fun and interaction for kids, including our Illuminate Elgin interactive light board and our piñata smash! Savor authentic Mexican food, sip on ice-cold micheladas, or cool down with a refreshing paleta. Plenty of local and popular food vendors will be joining us in the celebration.

Dance the night away with live performances by Banda Los Sebastianes, Estilo Al Norte, La Sonora Dinamita, and DJ Eddy P will be emceeing and spinning tunes between sets.

VIP LOUNGE AREA

Upgrade your Fiesta de Elgin experience with exclusive access to our VIP Lounge Area, designed for comfort, convenience, and unbeatable views! Purchase your VIP ticket during our early bird special for $25 from August 25th to August 29th. After the early bird special they will increase to $40. Get them before they sellout! If tickets remain available, they will be sold on site. A ticket is required for each guest age 12 and up entering the VIP area. Children under 12 are free but must be accompanied by an adult with a VIP ticket. Maximum of 2 children's tickets per VIP ticket. Your VIP ticket includes the following:

  • Reserved bar access, lawn seating and restrooms
  • Special michelada offerings provided by Pinches Miches only available at VIP
    • Cevicheladas: classic micheladas topped with mango ceviche
    • Yummycheladas: classic michelada topped with gomitas enchilados
  • Elevated viewing deck with umbrellas and ambient lighting and prime views of the stage and fireworks
  • Chairs are NOT included- please bring your own

EL GRITO AND FIREWORKS

El Grito- the traditional call for independence- will be a powerful moment of unity and pride as we honor the spirit of Mexican heritage. Then, we will light up the sky with a spectacular fireworks show to welcome the headliner who will close out the night!
